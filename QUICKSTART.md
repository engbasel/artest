## ðŸ“± Using the App

### Step 1: Choose Your 3D Model
- Tap **"Choose 3D Model"** on the home screen
- Select a `.glb`, `.gltf`, or `.obj` file from your device
- Supported formats only!

### Step 2: Launch AR
- Tap **"Launch AR Experience"**
- Grant camera permission when prompted
- Point your camera at a flat surface

### Step 3: Customize
- **Change Colors**: Swipe through the color picker at the bottom
- **Rotate**: Move your device around the model
- **Scale**: Pinch to zoom in/out
- **Move**: Drag the model to reposition

### Step 4: Switch Models (Optional)
- In AR view, tap the 3D icon (bottom left)
- Choose a different model
- AR will reload automatically

## ðŸŽ¯ Tips for Best Experience

1. **Good Lighting**: Use in well-lit environments
2. **Flat Surfaces**: Point camera at tables, floors, or walls
3. **Model Size**: Keep files under 50MB
4. **Movement**: Move slowly for better tracking
